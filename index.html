Biostatistical Methodology

方法學基石 — 多階段疾病自然史的隨機與貝氏模型

Methodological Foundation — Stochastic & Bayesian Models of Disease Natural History

貫穿所有應用研究的核心，是陳教授在生物統計方法學上的原創貢獻——以隨機過程、馬可夫與貝氏模型刻畫疾病自健康、臨床前期、症狀期至死亡的多階段自然史，並校正前導期偏差、病程差異偏差與過度偵測偏差，使無法進行隨機試驗的篩檢得以進行嚴謹的實證評估。這套方法學是上述各項應用研究共同的理論基礎。

Underlying every applied study is an original contribution to biostatistical methodology — stochastic, Markov and Bayesian models that characterize the multistate natural history of disease from health through pre-clinical and symptomatic phases to death, correcting lead-time, length and over-detection biases so that screening can be rigorously evaluated where randomized trials are impossible. This methodology is the shared theoretical basis of all the applied research below.
